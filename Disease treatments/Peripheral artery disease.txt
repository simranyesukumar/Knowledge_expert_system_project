Prevention:
Get plenty of physical activity to help prevent PAD or improve symptoms of PAD.
Do not use tobacco. 
Smoking increases the risk of PAD and makes PAD symptoms worse. 
Control high blood pressure and manage high blood cholesterol and diabetes.

Medications for  Peripheral Artery Disease?
Antihypertensive medication like Diovan or Benicar to lower blood pressure.
Statins to lower cholesterol.
Antiplatelets like aspirin or clopidogrel to prevent blood clots.
Medications like alpha-glucosidase inhibitors to control blood sugar.
